About Us

We works for the flood affected people in remote area of Bangladesh. Bangladesh is a land of river. Every year most of the remote area is go under water due heavy rain fall and rivers over flow. We work with this surviving people. We try to reach door to door. We have a team of some good voluteer who work hard to help these people.

How do we works ? We......

  • Receive inquries about the needs of the survivals
  • Raise fund for food and shelter for flood affected family
  • Collect data about the flood affected area
  • Reach door to door to provide medicine
  • Reach door to door to supply drinking water
  • Conduct rescue operation in remote area to save life
